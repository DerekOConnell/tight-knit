Support Local Businesses

Many small and independent local businesses are facing financial hardship due to COVID-19.

It's Time to Come Together

As a citizen of Vancouver it is our responsibility to support our local small and independent businesses and help them weather the storm of COVID-19. This site is intended to be a resource for finding and connecting with these businesses in our local community and learning ways that we can provide ongoing patronage to help them get through this tough time.

Supporting Vancouver businesses helps support jobs for people like you and I. As a tight-knit community we can come together in a variety of ways that can sustain local jobs and businesses.

Tell me more...

In order to slow the spread of the Coronavirus, the local government is advising restaurants, bars, cafes, nightclubs, movie theaters, small theater houses, and concert venues to close.

While this is prudent from a public health perspective, these closures will have a real and immediate economic impact to small and independent businesses across many industries. It could mean having to close temporarily, lay off staff or even close for good.

The economic impacts of the Coronavirus will be comparable to a very severe recession. We are already seeing local businesses shutter completely as they know they don't have the financial backing to survive this pandemic.
